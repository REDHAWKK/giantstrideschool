Frequently Asked Questions

Our regular school hours are from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM for all classes. We also offer after-school care until 4:00 PM for working parents.

Our admission process includes:
1. Submission of application form
2. Entrance assessment (for certain classes)
3. Interview with parents and child
4. Payment of fees upon acceptance

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including football, basketball, swimming, chess club, music, drama, coding club, art, and more.

Yes, we have a safe and reliable school bus service that covers most areas in the city. Our buses are equipped with seat belts and are supervised by trained bus monitors.

We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio to ensure personalized attention. For Nursery classes, the ratio is 1:10, while for Primary classes it's 1:15. We also have teaching assistants in all classes.
