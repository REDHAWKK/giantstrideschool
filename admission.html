Admission Process

Home / Admission

How To Apply

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form or visit the school to collect a physical form. Required documents include:

  • Birth certificate
  • Recent passport photograph
  • Previous school report (if applicable)
  • Medical records
2

Entrance Assessment

For admission into certain classes, your child will be required to take an age-appropriate assessment to evaluate their readiness. This helps us understand how we can best support their learning journey.

3

Interview

After the assessment, we invite both parents and the prospective student for an interview with our Head of School. This helps us get to know your family better and ensures we're a good fit for each other.

4

Acceptance & Enrollment

Successful applicants will receive an acceptance letter. To secure your child's place, you'll need to complete the enrollment process by submitting all required documents and paying the necessary fees.

Scholarship Opportunities

At Giant Stride School, we believe in rewarding excellence and supporting families who demonstrate financial need. We offer several scholarship opportunities:

  • Talent-Based Scholarship: For students demonstrating exceptional talent in sports, arts, or other areas.
