Our Story

Founded in 2005, Giant Stride School began as a small learning center with a vision to provide quality education that balances academic excellence with character development.

Today, we stand proud as a leading primary school with state-of-the-art facilities, qualified teachers, and a curriculum that prepares children for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our alumni have gone on to excel in various secondary schools and universities, a testament to the strong foundation we provide.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and moral values, equipping students with the skills and confidence to become responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be the premier primary school recognized for developing well-rounded individuals who excel academically, socially, and morally, making positive contributions to society.

Our Educational Approach

Holistic Curriculum

We combine the national curriculum with innovative teaching methods and extracurricular activities to ensure all-round development.

Child-Centered Learning

Our teaching methods are tailored to each child's learning style, ensuring no child is left behind.

Practical Learning

We emphasize hands-on experiences and real-world applications to make learning engaging and meaningful.
